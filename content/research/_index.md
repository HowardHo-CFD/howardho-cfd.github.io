<div style="text-align: justify;">
Dr. Howard Haonan Ho's research focuses on computational fluid dyanmcis, Active Flow Control (AFC), and Aerodynamics. Over the past 9+ years, he has conducted fundamental and applied numerical investigations using high-performance computing (HPC), Large Eddy Simulation (LES), and RANS/URANS models. His primary work investigates 3D synthetic jet interactions in crossflow boundary layers, cylinder wake flow control, and physics-informed neural network applications. He has authored over 15 peer-reviewed publications across top fluid mechanics journals and international conferences, including <i>Physics of Fluids</i> and the <i>International Symposium on Turbulence and Shear Flow Phenomena (TSFP)</i>.
