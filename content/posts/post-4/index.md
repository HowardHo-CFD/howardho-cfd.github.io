I've been reflecting on the impact of **Piazza** (an online forum-style learning management system) on the Dynamics course I've been instructing, which has an enrollment of 650 students. 

We used Piazza primarily for addressing student concerns regarding homework and exam preparation. Over the span of 100 days, we received a total of **1,233 posts** (excluding follow-ups) — a **61% increase** compared to the previous year. Notably, this heightened engagement correlated with a **20% increase in final exam results**. The figure above displays the individual posts per day throughout the semester, including follow-up discussions and instructor responses.

---

### Key Performance Metrics & Efficiency

To match the rise in questions, our team significantly improved response efficiency:

* ⚡ **Average Response Time:** Reduced from **28 minutes** in 2023 down to just **6 minutes** this year.
* 💬 **Instructor Contributions:** I personally provided **1,120 responses**, contributing to **45% overall input** alongside co-instructor efforts.
* 🎯 **Targeted Conceptual Mastery:** Two notoriously complex concepts — **relative motion** and **vibrations** — were handled much more effectively by students, largely due to rapid, structured discussion on the platform.

---

The positive impact of Piazza in a large-scale post-secondary environment cannot be overstated. When properly supported by teaching staff, interactive discussion tools have the potential to revolutionize student learning experiences. I eagerly anticipate seeing Piazza implemented even more widely across different courses and institutions in the future!
