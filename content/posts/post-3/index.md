<div style="text-align: justify;">

Happy to present some of our latest work at **TSFP-13** (*Thirteenth International Symposium on Turbulence and Shear Flow Phenomena*) in Montreal last week. The presentation included both experimental and numerical work done on applying Synthetic Jet Actuators (SJA) on Active Flow Control, we concluded that an array of circular SJAs can effective suppress flow separation even without jet to jet interactions.
