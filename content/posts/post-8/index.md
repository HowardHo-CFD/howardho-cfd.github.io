Excited to share that I presented at the Thousand Islands Meeting (TIM) on Fluids this past weekend! 🌟

A huge thank you to the organizers for putting together such a fantastic conference and to everyone for their presentations!

This TIM I presented my recent LES case study on flow past a circular cylinder at a Reynolds number of 3900. A recorded version of the presentation can be found on my academic channel: 🎥 https://lnkd.in/eG7MAkuJ
